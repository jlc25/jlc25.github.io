Context

This project was our project for our first Hackathon.

Introduction

A lot of people struggle coming up with their own passwords in a way that is safe when creating their accounts for various websites. We tried to simplify their process by making this generator. The user only needs to click on one button to get their newly generated password. They can also adjust the length of it using a slider.
